## Conclusion

The NPA ECM system has a solid foundation with excellent implementations for Officer, AGM, and MD roles. The primary focus should be on enhancing the GM/ED, Registry, and System Admin dashboards to provide comprehensive functionality across all organizational levels. The recommended improvements will create a more cohesive, efficient, and user-friendly system that supports the full spectrum of NPA operations.

**Next Steps**: Begin with Phase 1 implementations, focusing on the GM/ED dashboard as it serves a critical role in the organizational hierarchy and currently has the most significant functionality gaps.
