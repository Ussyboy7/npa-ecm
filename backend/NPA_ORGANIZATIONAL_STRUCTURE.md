# NPA Organizational Structure for ECM System

## Hierarchy Overview

```
Managing Director (MD)
├── Executive Director, Finance & Administration
├── Executive Director, Marine & Operations
├── Executive Director, Engineering & Technical Services
└── Corporate Services (Direct to MD)
```

## Document Access Levels by Position

### Level 1 - Confidential (MD, EDs only)
- Board documents
- Strategic plans
- Executive decisions

### Level 2 - Restricted (GMs and above)
- Divisional reports
- Budget proposals
- Cross-divisional memos

### Level 3 - Internal (AGMs and above)
- Department reports
- Operational documents
- Standard memos

### Level 4 - General (All staff)
- Circulars
- Announcements
- Public documents

This structure will be used for:
- User role assignment
- Document access control
- Workflow routing
- Department-based filtering
- Approval hierarchies
